About Me

I am Wendi, a graduate student majored in telecommunications from Beijing Jiaotong University. Currently, I am working as a part-time "Networking Software Engineer" intern at China Mobile. My work focuses primarily on the research of coexisting issues among 2G, 3G and LTE networks. I also maintain a web service for company's internal use and generate some automation tests. I like software and I cannot wait to witness the amazing things it will bring that today seem unimaginably fantastic.
